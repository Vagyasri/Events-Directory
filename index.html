France Directory works in four simple steps.

1 - SEARCH:

Search for events in France. It's simple, on France Directory enter the name of a event you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively, click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all the events.

2 - LOCATE:

Get to see the venue of the event by clicking on the Event profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

1 - REGISTER:

Register for the event you want to be part of. Click on the REGISTER button to get the registration form.

2 - ENJOY:

You will get a confirmation E-mail in your registered E-mail id along with the details of your booking.
